Parking

You must use a PARKING PERMIT (this will be added before the event) for both days at BSides Austin. Closer to the event dates, we will provide a permit that you can download and PRINT, then place it in plain view on your dashboard.

Without this parking permit on your dash you will get a ticket.

We have been advised that UT security is very diligent about monitoring the parking when there is a big event like ours. In fact, they have also been known to ticket if you have a tire on a parking space line or on any grass.

* We have no power to help you get out of a ticket if you get one.

You can avoid the whole parking mess:

The Austin MetroRapid bus stop is located on Exploration Way on the west side of the Commons Building and offers transit daily along a 15-mile route through Austin. For more information, please see: Cap Metro Route 803.

Hotels

If you're coming into Austin for our event and need a room, you'll find a list of hotels located within a three-mile radius of the conference center: https://commons.utexas.edu/meetings-and-events/area-hotels
