Calling all speakers!

Call for Speakers

Interested in speaking at BSides Austin 2022 on December 2nd? Our virtual format means you can present to our community of security professionals and students from anywhere.

  • Speaking slots are for 30 minutes (the default) or 60 minutes (please build in time within that for Q&A). If you need more than 30 minutes, let us know using the “other notes” field at the end of the submission form.
  • We are looking for high-value presentations with information that attendees can take back to their jobs and implement immediately.
  • We prefer talks that are more technical in nature.
  • We will not accept talks that are about companies or products (no sales or executive talks, please).
  • This call for papers will close at midnight CST on Sunday, October 23, 2022. We will notify accepted speakers by October 26th, and we will require your confirmation by October 29th.

Please submit your proposal here. If you have any questions, please email us. We look forward to receiving your talk submission.
