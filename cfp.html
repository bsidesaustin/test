Calling all speakers!

Call for Speakers

Interested in speaking at BSides Austin 2024 on December 5th or 6th? This is the right place!

  • Speaking slots are for 55 or 25 minutes (please build in time within that for Q&A). We are scheduling 5 minutes between talks.
  • We are looking for high-value presentations with information that attendees can take back to their jobs and implement immediately.
  • We prefer talks that are more technical in nature.
  • We will not accept talks that are about companies or products (no sales talks or pitches, please).
  • This call for papers will close at midnight CST on October 3rd, 2024. We will notify accepted speakers by October 8th, and we will require your confirmation by October 13th.

Please submit your proposal here. If you have any questions, please email us. We look forward to receiving your talk submission.
