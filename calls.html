Calling all speakers, trainers and volunteers!

Call for Speakers

BSides Austin will take place April 9th and 10th in Austin, Texas.

    • Speaking slots are 50 minutes (please build in time within your 50 minutes for Q&A).
    • We prefer talks that are information rich.
    • Please do not submit talks that are about companies or products.
    • Please note that because BSides conferences are required to be low cost for their attendees, we do not have funds to cover travel or honorariums for speakers.

We look forward to your talk submission. Please submit your talk here. If you have any questions, please email us.

This call for papers will close at midnight CST on February 14, 2020. Accepted speakers will be notified by February 19th. We must receive confirmation by February 26th.

Call for Trainers

BSides Austin will take place April 9th and 10th in Austin, Texas.

    • Training submissions can be from 2 hours to 2 days.
    • 4-hour, full day and two day talks are likely to be on the days prior to the conference, i.e. April 7th and 8th.
    • Please note that because BSides conferences are required to be low cost for their attendees, we do not have funds to cover travel or honorariums for trainers. The training are split with the instructor(s) 50 trainer/50 BSides.

We look forward to your training submission. Please submit your talk here. If you have any questions, please email us.

This call for training will close at midnight CST on February 14, 2020. Accepted speakers will be notified by February 19th. We must receive confirmation by February 26th.

Call for Volunteers

BSides Austin will take place April 9th and 10th in Austin, Texas.

Without volunteers, no BSides events would possible. You can make a difference by getting involved.

    • Volunteers work both days all day. We do try to match volunteers with tracks they are interested in and try to coordinate time off so some talks can be viewed.
    • Volunteers receive a volunteer t-shirt and meals

Please submit your application here. If you have any questions, please email us.

We will notify applicants mid-March about selection. Thank you!
