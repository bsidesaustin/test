Calling all speakers, trainers, and volunteers!

ALL Calls are CLOSED

Speaking opportunities for BSides Austin are for the dates of the conference: Thursday and Friday, April 9th and 10th, in Austin, Texas.

  • Speaking slots are 50 minutes long (please build in time within your 50 minutes for Q&A).
  • We prefer talks that are information rich.
    • Please do not submit talks that are about companies or products.
  • Speakers will be registered for the conference without fee.
  • Please note that because BSides conferences are required to be low cost for their attendees, we do not have funds to cover travel or honorariums for speakers.

Please submit your proposal here. If you have any questions, please email us. We look forward to receiving your talk submission.

This call for papers will close at midnight CST on February 14, 2020. Accepted speakers will be notified by February 19th. We must receive confirmation by February 26th.

Call for Trainings

For two days prior to the BSides Austin conference, we will offer trainings: Tuesday and Wednesday, April 7th and 8th, in Austin.

  • Trainings submitted can have half-day, full-day, or two-day running times.
  • Please note that because BSides conferences are required to be low cost for their attendees, we do not have funds to cover travel or honorariums for trainers. Fees collected for trainings will be split with the instructor(s): 50% trainer/50% BSides Austin.

Please submit your proposal here. If you have any questions, please email us. We look forward to receiving your training submission.

This call for training will close at midnight CST on February 14, 2020. Accepted trainers will be notified by February 19th. We must receive confirmation by February 26th.

Call for Volunteers

Without volunteers, no BSides events would be possible. If you're the kind of person that likes to be part of the crew at events, please sign up to get involved.

  • Volunteers work both days all day. We do try to match volunteers with tracks they are interested in and try to coordinate time off so some talks can be viewed.
  • Volunteers receive a volunteer t-shirt and lunch.

Please submit your application here. If you have any questions, please email us.

We will notify applicants mid-March about selection. Thank you!
