Sponsors

BSides Austin provides an environment for our sponsors to directly engage with key members of the Texas security community. Our attendees are looking for more than just information; they are looking to make professional and vendor connections they may call upon throughout the rest of their careers. We understand that every organization considering sponsorship will have different goals and priorities, which is why we offer several levels of participation. Please contact us if interested in being sent our 2020 sponsorship kit.
