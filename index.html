Look for us in 2022!

We hate to say it, but we just can't make BSides Austin happen in 2021. Our venue closed, and we have yet to find a suitable replacement that would give us enough time to orchestrate the conference. At this point, we are hoping to present a face-to-face event in late spring 2022. Please watch our twitter feed for updates @bsidesaustin. Thank you for your continued interest and support.

BSides Austin provides a wonderful environment for our sponsors to directly engage with key members of the Texas security community. Our attendees are looking for more than just information; they are looking to make professional and vendor connections they may call upon throughout the rest of their careers. We understand that every organization considering sponsorship will have different goals and priorities, which is why we offer several levels of participation.
